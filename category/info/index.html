SEPTR is a diverse group of organized mountain biking enthusiasts who share a common love of the outdoors and the desire to promote our sport. Our membership comprises all levels and disciplines of mountain biking in the southeastern Pennsylvania area. SEPTR members of all ages enjoy advocating, volunteering, building and maintaining trails as much as they enjoy riding them.
